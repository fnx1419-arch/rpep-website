Program Structure

A structured pathway designed to build radiology expertise step by step.

Foundation Phase

Build strong fundamentals in radiology through structured resources, guided reading, and beginner-friendly learning materials that prepare you for hospital exposure.

Program Schedule

Phase Duration Activities Outcomes
Foundation Phase 4 weeks Introductory lectures, online modules, anatomy review Core knowledge established
Hospital Shadowing 6 weeks Clinical observation, department rotations, case discussions Clinical exposure gained
Workshops & Skills 5 weeks Hands-on workshops, image interpretation, case studies Practical skills developed
Advanced Exploration 5 weeks Subspecialty exposure, research projects, mentorship Career pathway defined
Total Duration 20 weeks (5 months)

About RPEP

"The Radiology Pathway Experience Program prepares students for future careers in radiology through exposure, mentorship, and structured guidance."

Mission
Vision
Values

Mission

To provide aspiring medical students with comprehensive exposure to radiology as a career path, offering hands-on experience, mentorship, and structured guidance that bridges the gap between academic learning and professional practice.

Vision

To become the leading pathway program in Oman that cultivates the next generation of skilled radiologists, increasing awareness and accessibility of radiology careers while contributing to the advancement of healthcare excellence in the region.

Values

We are committed to guidance through expert mentorship, professionalism in all interactions, equity in access to opportunities, continuous growth through learning, and excellence in preparing students for successful radiology careers.
